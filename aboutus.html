About Us

NextGen is a full-service interactive agency that helps clients succeed online by creating award-winning digital solutions. One Solution's services start with a digital strategy that evolves into a complete solution including creativity, technology, and marketing. One Solution works with small to medium sized businesses, as well as leading brands, to create comprehensive and result-driven online marketing campaigns. In addition, One Solution's technology offerings, which range from e-commerce and content management systems to social networking platforms, allow companies to efficiently run their business while maintaining a high ROI and growth.

NextGen is a privately held company founded in1990. We are headquartered in pune. and have our offices in Canada, USA, Egypt, Dubai & Gulf countries. Our clients cover a wide-range of industries and are located all over the world. Read further for more information or visit our portfolio to see examples of our work.
